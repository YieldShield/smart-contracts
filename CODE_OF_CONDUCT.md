# Code of Conduct

YieldShield aims to keep project spaces respectful, focused, and safe for
contributors.

## Expected Behavior

- Be constructive and specific.
- Assume good intent while staying clear about risks and tradeoffs.
- Respect different experience levels, backgrounds, and communication styles.
- Keep technical disagreement about the work, not the person.
- Handle security-sensitive information responsibly.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Sexualized language or unwanted attention.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Publicly disclosing vulnerabilities before maintainers have had a reasonable
  chance to triage and remediate them.
- Repeated disruption of issues, reviews, or maintainer workflows.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues or pull requests;
or restrict participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.

To report conduct concerns, contact the maintainers privately. For security
vulnerabilities, use the process in `SECURITY.md`.
